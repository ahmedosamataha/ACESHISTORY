ABOUT US

ACES stands for "Annual Conference for Engineering Students", It was founded in 2004 being the 1st student activity in FOE ASU with the idea of reducing the gap between the academic life and job market need.

Vision

MAKING A COMMUNITY OF INFLUENTIAL LEADERS.

Mission

RAISING THE CAPABILITIES OF THE ENGINEERING STUDENTS.

Values

"TRUST, SIMPLICITY, PAY IT FORWARD & INTEGRITY EXCELLENCE"



WORKSHOPS

ACES Workshops was the first main event in ACES was published in 2004.This event concentrates on the individuals development non-technically to enhance their skills, raise and enlighten their knowledge by attending well-organized five sessions and picking up professional case studies delivered by committed moderators.



ACG ACES Career Gate

ACES Career Gate was founded in 2012, It was online, by 2014 ACG became a Seminar, and in 2015 ACG became on ground. Since 2016 ACG became online again.

What diffrentiates ACG from other Job Sites and Job fairs?
ACG concentrates on the Engineering Community, The selection in ACG is based on the quality of the appliers, Unlike other Job sites, a job might have 20,000+ appliers, therefore the selection may not be fair due to the high number of appliers.



EXPO

ACES Expo is the main technical event of ACES, it was founded in 2014, at the very begining it was called "The Idea". Participants were supposed to come up with an idea (case study) and the idea would be offered to the companies, the companies had the choice to agree or refuse.
Then, this system changed to be even better; ACES now agrees with the companies on specific topics (case studies), and recruits participants to work on the chosen topics.

ACES Co-operates with numerous companies such as P&G, PepsiCo, and many more.
Because of EXPO, ACES has a very effective role in raising the technical capabilities of Engineering Students.
